== Description ==
Burq enables you to offer commission-free on-demand/same-day delivery to your customers, within minutes! After installing, your customers will have the option to choose delivery during their checkout. Our network compiles drivers from multiple delivery providers. When it’s time to ship out a product, we dispatch the best driver for the job—no long waits or headaches. Instead, we allow you to spend your time doing what you love: building your business.

#### **Key Features**

1.  Hassle-free on-demand and same-day delivery
2.  Request immediate delivery or schedule future orders
3.  Up-to-the-minute tracking updates, available via web and SMS
4.  Commission-free 
5.  Seamless integration within minutes
